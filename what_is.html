What Is Project Plutus?

According to Greek legend, Plutus is the god of wealth. In Agrarian Greece he was at first associated purely with bounty of rich harvests. Later he came to represent wealth in more general terms. He was believed come to poverty stricken societies and revive them economically. Project Plutus is an initiative which creates a direct link between individuals and non-profit microfinancing organizations, so people can donate their spare change to help empower poverty stricken societies just like Plutus

We were founded in 2015 by a group of students who were driven to bring non-profit organizations and individuals closer together through technological advances.
