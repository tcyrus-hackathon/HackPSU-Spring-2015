Why Micro-Financing?

Micro-Financing companies have the abilities to find people who need money most. The people who benefit from your contributions are people who are driven and who have plans for how the micro-loan will better their future. 100% of the money the non-profit micro-financing organizations receive goes to people in need in the form of a micro-loan, and once the small loan is repaid the money goes to a new family in need. Non-profit micro-financing organizations such as Kiva, have the resources and manpower to interact with people all around the world. Unfortunately, one of the biggest obstacles for the micro-financing organizations is that they don't have enough money to create the much needed micro-loans. This is how Project Plutus helps!

Supporting Businesses

Farmers face unpredictable conditions, weather and market prices. Your support keeps their crops growing and their families stable. Your contributions can also help businessmen in developing countries become more sustainable. Thomas, a 55 year old farmer in Honduras wants to build an organic greenhouse to support his family of five.

Supporting Education

Micro-finances give vulnerable students the opportunity to attend vocational programs and higher educational institutions. Ricardo the founder of Icfotep, a school in Haiti destroyed after an earthquake, receives micro-loans to invest in much needed academic supplies.
